With explainable AI and gamified progression, Tradon isn’t just a tool — it’s your trading companion.

How to Get Started

FAQs

Looking for answers?

What is Tradon?

Tradon is an AI-powered trading assistant you can train and evolve. Each Tradon adapts to your style, offering personalized token recommendations, analysis reports, and performance tracking. You create it, guide its growth, and reap the rewards through the $TRDN ecosystem.

How do I get started with Tradon?

Simply access the Tradon Mini-App on Telegram, launch your agent, and begin training it using market charts, strategic feedback, and performance missions. No prior AI or trading experience is required — just your insights and curiosity.

What is $TRDN used for?

$TRDN is the native utility token of the Tradon ecosystem. It's used to create and upgrade agents, access premium reports, participate in agent battles, and earn rewards through ranking competitions or staking mechanisms.

Can I evolve or upgrade my Tradon?

Yes. Your Tradon gains experience and levels through training and successful predictions. You can evolve it into advanced forms like Tradon Prime or Tradon Oracle, unlocking new abilities and higher-ranking potential in global leaderboards.
